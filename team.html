John G. Prisk | Co-Founder, Managing Partner

John co-founded MIG and serves as Managing Partner. He has a diverse professional background ranging from civil engineering and real estate finance to project management and private equity. At MIG, John is responsible for investment strategy/ideation, asset/portfolio management, finance-related functions, research/analytics, and investor relations.

In prior assignments, John has raised capital on behalf of real estate sponsors at a boutique investment banking house and worked as a quantitative analyst for two publicly-traded institutional real estate companies.

John graduated cum laude with a Bachelor of Science from Drexel University and is an active member of the Urban Land Institute.

Michael Boczar | Co-Founder, Executive Director

Mike co-founded MIG and serves as Executive Director. He is primarily responsible for corporate operations and project management. He also plays a pivotal role in the firm’s acquisition, due diligence, and business development activities.

In previous roles, Mike specialized in the management of commercial construction projects. His responsibilities included oversight of preconstruction and approvals, procurement, subcontractor buyout and field management, project scheduling, value engineering, and project accounting.

Mike holds a Bachelor of Science degree in construction management, real estate and finance from Drexel University. He currently sits on the Board of Directors for the Construction Financial Management Association's Philadelphia Chapter. He is also an advisor to the Drexel University chapter of Construction Management Association of America’s Drexel University Chapter.

Matthew Cowan | Senior Vice President

Matthew Cowan joined MIG in 2015 and serves as Senior Vice President, where he is tasked with managing the firm's design and approvals strategy. Matt is also instrumental in supporting our strategic planning, asset management, due diligence, and special projects efforts.

In addition to his design prowess, Matt brings significant financial analysis and risk management experience from a successful fifteen year career in the hedge fund space. In that time, he managed a $150MM trading platform based in Bermuda, while employed by behalf one of the original quantitative/algorithmic funds.

In 2011, Matt decided to drop out of Wall Street to pursue his long-standing interest in design and the built environment at the University of Michigan. There, he completed a Master in Urban Planning degree, as well as a Graduate Certificate in Real Estate Development.

Matt is a Chartered Financial Analyst, Chartered Professional Accountant, and a Certified Management Account. He holds a Bachelor of Business Administration with Honors from Wilfrid Laurier University, Ontario, Canada.
